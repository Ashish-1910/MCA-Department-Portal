Master of Computer Applications (General)

Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at UIC is a comprehensive 2-year postgraduate program designed to empower students with modern computing skills and specializations. Students can choose areas such as System Programming, Database Management, Networking, Web Design, and Artificial Intelligence. The program integrates practical exposure with theoretical learning, ensuring our graduates excel in the global IT industry.

The curriculum is updated to meet global standards and focuses on emerging technologies such as Cloud Computing, Android Development, JAVA, Oracle, .NET, PHP, and CISCO. UIC stands among the best MCA colleges in India, providing a balance of innovation, creativity, and technical mastery.
